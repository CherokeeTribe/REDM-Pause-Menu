RULES
1) This Server is Roleplay (RP)
We welcome players who are eager to engage in imaginative, creative scenarios and immersive ROLEPLAY. This is not a server for those seeking a casual, non-RP experience akin to [GTA Online]. Before entering, you must read and understand the server rules. Familiarity with these rules will help you avoid mistakes and enhance your RP experience. Voice chat is mandatory, and we expect clear and respectful communication. Voice changers are permitted within reasonable limits as long as they maintain immersion.

2) Respect and Courtesy
Respect and courtesy are the cornerstones of this community. Bullying or harassment of any kind will not be tolerated, especially toward new players. Being a veteran player does not grant you the right to be rude, use inappropriate language, or act disrespectfully. Any breaches of this rule will result in serious consequences. Remember, our server welcomes players of all ages, so ensure your in-character actions and dialogue remain respectful, even within RP scenarios.

3) Out of Character (OOC)
Avoid stepping out of character during active RP scenarios. Continue roleplaying to the end, even if mistakes occur. Afterward, you can file a report explaining your concerns. Reports without valid reasons will be ignored. For technical issues or server-related queries, please contact staff via Discord instead of disrupting in-game RP by asking players.

4) Power Gaming
Power gaming is strictly forbidden. Examples include: - Taking actions your character would realistically be unable to perform (e.g., surviving impossible injuries without consequences). - Exploiting game mechanics to gain unfair advantages in RP (e.g., ignoring injuries after a high-speed crash). Always strive for realism and fairness in your actions.

5) Random Death Match (RDM) and Vehicle Death Match (VDM)
Killing or harming other players without valid RP scenarios is prohibited (RDM/VDM). Robbing someone and then killing them without reason is also forbidden. Any such actions will be met with disciplinary measures.

6) Value of Life (VoL)
Treat your character’s life as precious and unique. Act as though you only have one life to live. Reckless or nonsensical actions that devalue your character’s life will not be tolerated.

7) New Life Rule (NLR)
If your character dies, their memory is erased regarding the events leading to their death. Revenge is only allowed if a witness provides credible information, and even then, they cannot describe the exact circumstances of your death.

8) Law Enforcement and Emergency Services Interaction
Avoid intentionally provoking law enforcement or emergency services without a meaningful RP reason. Stealing or using their vehicles is only permitted if it is part of a prearranged RP scenario.

9) Meta Gaming
Using out-of-character (OOC) information to influence in-character (IC) decisions is strictly prohibited. Sharing RP-related information via Discord, streams, or other external platforms undermines the integrity of the RP experience and will result in severe penalties.

10) Combat Logging
Logging out during an active RP scenario is prohibited, whether your character is alive or dead. Disconnecting to avoid consequences disrupts RP and is unacceptable.

11) Historical Accuracy and Immersion
This server is set in 1899. While some creative liberties are allowed, strive to maintain historical accuracy in character behavior, dialogue, attire, and technology. Breaks in immersion (e.g., modern language or unrealistic character concepts) detract from the experience and will be addressed.

12) RP Creativity and Initiative
Players are encouraged to create meaningful and engaging scenarios. Avoid relying on repetitive or generic actions. Bring depth to your character and enrich the server’s RP environment through thoughtful storylines and interactions.

13) Animal Roleplay (Rare occasions)
When roleplaying as animals, maintain realistic behaviors. Unrealistic actions, such as speaking as an animal or acting outside the natural capabilities of the animal, are not permitted.

14) Firearm and Weapon Usage
Weapons should only be used appropriately within RP scenarios. Brandishing or discharging weapons randomly or without context detracts from the immersive experience and is forbidden.
